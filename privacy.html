Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 20, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how CampusLink collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We collect your name, university email, and a password when you create an account.

Engagement Data: We track posts you like, users you follow, and comments you make to personalize your feed.

Log Data: We collect standard browser information to improve site performance and security.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to facilitate connectivity between students, display relevant university content, and protect our platform from unauthorized access.

3. Information Sharing

CampusLink does not sell your personal data to third parties. We only share information when required by law or to protect our users.

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and Firebase's secure authentication systems to protect your account. However, no electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can update your profile, delete your posts, or close your account at any time via the settings page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any concerns about your privacy, please send a message to the CampusLink Admin team.
